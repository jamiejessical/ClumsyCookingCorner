FISH

fishing for compliments!

There are a ton of fish in the sea, and some of them end up on our plates. Cod, salmon, tilapia, tuna... you know, the usual suspects. Pick one, cook it up, or don't. Whatever floats your boat, I guess!

*quick tip*

Don't forget, fish cooks fast, so keep an eye on it to avoid overcooking.
